Our Devices

Desktop

Even in our buzzing mobile world, I’m a fan of the stationary desktop computer—or at least the well-appointed desk. I understand how old-fashioned that sounds. After I effused about my desktop setup to a senior Silicon Valley exec a few months back, he asked if I also rode a horse and buggy.

Tablet

Tablet computer, computer that is intermediate in size between a laptop computer and a smartphone. Early tablet computers used either a keyboard or a stylus to input information, but these methods were subsequently displaced by touch screens.The precursors to the tablet computer were devices.

Mobile

Mobile Phone is often also called “cellular phone”. It is a device mainly used for a voice call. Presently technological advancements have made our life easy. Today, with the help of a mobile phone we can easily talk or video chat with anyone across the globe by just moving our fingers.
